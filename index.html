Summary

Before high school, I didn’t have much direction and was just adjusting to normal life after COVID. As a freshman, I explored both programming and health sciences to see where my interests lay. By sophomore year, I leaned toward programming, taking an Object-Oriented Programming class and joining activities like Codeathon, Drone Club, and CoderZ. Now, as a junior, I’m in a web design class, expanding my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Looking ahead, I plan to take more programming courses, continue participating in Codeathon, and rejoin Drone Club. My ultimate goal is to build a career in computer science.

Pre-High School:

Pre-high school, I had no plan for myself. I was just starting to get used to living normally (COVID). I was a a shell of my former self. I got my act together as I transitioned into high school.

Freshman Year:

Freshman year, I wasn’t sure what I wanted to be. As a result, I tried out both the programming and health pathways that the school had. I took Programming (CTE) (Mr. Dal) as well as Health Science and Technology (Ms. Meldrum).

Sophomore Year:

Sophomore year, I was leaning towards the programming pathway. As a result, I was in Mr Dal’s Object Oriented Programming class. This was the first year I participated in Codeathon. I was apart of the Drone Club and I did CoderZ.

Junior Year:

As a current junior, I am apart of Ms. Pathan’s Web Design class. I also participated in Codeathon. I learned about HTML, CSS, and now I’m learning about JavaScript.

My Future:

I plan on taking additional programming classes and I plan on participating in Codeathon again. I also plan on rejoining Drone Club as I didn’t have the time this year. My goal (career) is to have a career in computer science.
